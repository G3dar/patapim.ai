## Overview

Refer friends to PATAPIM and earn credit toward a free Pro license.

## How It Works

1. Share your referral link (available on the upgrade page at patapim.ai)
2. When someone signs up and purchases Pro using your link, you earn credit
3. Credits are auto-claimed when you log in to PATAPIM

## Tracking

Referrals are tracked through the patapim.ai website. The referred user enters your email during checkout, and the system records the referral.

## Earning Credit

Each successful referral earns you credit toward a free license. Credits accumulate and are applied to your account automatically.
